EMMET IS AMAZING!!!


Recently, I found out about an amazing plugin called Emmet. If you get annoyed typing out tag after tag in HTML, Emmet is the perfect solution. You simply type the name of the tag you want once, add any extra information such as the number of tags or any children tags, and press a button––I mapped mine to ',,'–– and voila. Your HTML tags are there, both opened and closed, ready for you to fill them with content. Plus, there's shortcuts to skip to the next spot to edit. If I have a bunch of list items I need to fill, it's as easy as pressing ',n' to jump to the middle of the next list item. The shortcuts can be whatever you map them to be, of course, but I found that the comma is the most convienent for me.
